\subsection{Turbulent Prandtl models}

The Turbulent Prandtl models, estimate the turbulent diffusivity coefficient $\alpha_t$ in the following way:

\begin{equation}
\alpha_t = \frac{\mu_t}{\rave{\rho}Pr_t}
\end{equation}
